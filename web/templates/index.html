How to use MKSDrive App

Enter username(Email id) and password and then click on "Proceed" button to login.
Optionally check the "Remember Me" box to store your credentials for next login.
At login for first time, the app will prompt to change your password. An otp will be sent to your registered email. Use this otp while changing the password.
If you have forgot password, click on "Forgot Password" button and then follow the steps as above.
Once logged in, you can view your profile by clicking "My Profile" icon on navigation view.
Also you can change your password from the menu at top right icon.\n\n\n
To view the files, click on "Goto Drive" button on the home screen.
Folders are represented with pastel yellow color background.
You can download a file by clicking on it. Also by long pressing you can request the file by email.
Folder request feature is currently not available.
